About Us

Jai Prabha Textiles has been a trusted name in the field of industrial filter cloth manufacturing since 1973. Over the years, we have expanded to become one of the largest manufacturers of dust filter bags in India. Our wide range of products includes woven fabrics, non-woven fabrics, filter media, dust collection bags, non-woven filter fabrics, pulse jet filter bags, envelope filter bags, and F.B.D. bags.

Under the leadership of Mr. V.P. Aggarwal (B.Tech in Textile Engineering with specialization in Industrial Textiles), we have built a strong reputation for quality and reliability. Our sister concern, Total Filter Solutions, is also a leading name in the industry.

With 34 years of experience, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and a highly skilled team, we ensure the fastest deliveries and competitive pricing. We believe in stringent quality control at every stage of production using superior raw materials like polypropylene, cotton, glasswares, and polystyrene.
